    Maple

    Maple is a commercial computer algebra system developed and sold commercially by Maplesoft, a software company based in Waterloo, Ontario, Canada. The University of Sheffield has a site license that allows all staff and students to use it on University and personal machines.

    • The documentation for Maple on Sheffield's High Performance Computing System, Iceberg, is at http://docs.iceberg.shef.ac.uk/en/latest/software/apps/r.html
    • High Performance Computing with Maple, Part 1 - A detailed tutorial on how to use Maple with Sheffield's HPC cluster
    • Download Maple - Maple is available for Sheffield University staff and students from the CiCS download site
